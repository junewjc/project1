About Us

Latin Dance Studio was formed in 2019 by a fun and dynamic group of dance enthusiasts who share the same zeal and love for dancing.

We aim to bring to you a brand new experience in learning dancing by offering the list of classes below:

  • Salsa
  • Bachata
  • Brazilian Zouk

In Latin Dance Studio, we believe firmly in teaching and dancing for the passion of dance. Our aim is to make every single student enjoy the beauty of dance.

If you are looking for the first step to kick start your dancing life, Latin Dance Studio is the place for you to experience FUN and EXCITEMENT through our dance courses!

Our Instructors

Brazilian Zouk

Willam & Paloma

William Teixeira and Paloma Alves are both from Brazil. They are well-known for their connection, musicality, and energy.

They are passionate about music and both work as DJs while giving workshops and performing at the biggest Zouk Congresses around the world.

Bachata

Kike & Nahir

Kike Utrera is from Spain and Nahir Romano is from Argentina. They are well-known to be pioneers in Bachata Fusion.

They have founded the Danzas School (Argentina) in 2010 and have performed and taught workshops in major bachata events worldwide.

Salsa

Karen & Ricardo

Karen Forcano and Ricardo Vega are both from Chile. They are well-known for the performances in America's Got Talent S12.

They have many accolades, such as two-time champions of World Cup World Latin Dance 2012-2013 and winners of the Argentina Open Salsa.
